## 10. Key Findings & Recommendations

### Strengths

1. **Strong Q4 growth** (+27.7%) demonstrates market demand
2. **Diverse coach team** now provides capacity and coverage
3. **Package sales** dominate - indicates customer commitment
4. **Growing customer base** with 131 unique customers in 10 months

### Opportunities

1. **Reduce 1-month churn** (66% of customers)
   - Implement follow-up program after first session
   - Offer incentive for second booking within 7 days
   - Consider starter package price optimization

2. **Increase session frequency** (currently 1.6-1.7 sessions/customer)
   - Promote bulk packages more aggressively
   - Introduce loyalty rewards for consistent bookings
   - Weekly practice sessions at lower rate

3. **Expand 2 PAX offerings** (only 16% of revenue)
   - Current premium (35-60%) may be too high
   - Promote "bring a friend" discounts
   - Group lesson options for beginners

4. **Optimize coach utilization**
   - BOSS underutilized in recent months
   - NOON appears to be part-time - consider expanding
   - Standardize scheduling across all coaches

5. **Address seasonal dips**
   - Jul-Sep and December show 30-40% drops
   - Consider promotional pricing during slow periods
   - Corporate packages for rainy season indoor golf

### Risks

1. **Coach concentration**: RATCHAVIN handles 44% of revenue - dependency risk
2. **High churn rate**: 66% single-month customers affects LTV
3. **Seasonal volatility**: Revenue swings of 40%+ between peak and trough months
