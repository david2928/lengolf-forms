### Club Rental Recommendations

#### ✅ RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

##### 1. Track Standard Club Usage
**Priority:** High | **Investment:** ฿0 | **Impact:** Better data

Currently, only premium rentals appear in POS. Add "Standard Club Usage" as ฿0 line item when staff checks out free clubs.

**Benefits:**
- Measure true attachment rate (how many customers use ANY clubs)
- Calculate accurate conversion rate to premium
- Identify peak demand times
- Forecast equipment needs

##### 2. Push Full-Day Tourist Packages
**Priority:** High | **Investment:** Time only | **Impact:** +฿3-5K/month

Your "Use at any course" angle is underutilized. Full-day rentals at ฿1,200 are your highest margin.

**Actions:**
- Partner with nearby hotels (Marriott, Sofitel, etc.)
- Add to Google Maps listing: "Golf club rental for Bangkok courses"
- Create dedicated landing page for tourists
- Offer delivery/pick-up service: **฿500** (hotel or central Bangkok locations)

**Target Keywords:**
- "rent golf clubs Bangkok"
- "golf club rental Thailand tourist"
- "hire golf clubs Bangkok course"

**Tourist Package with Delivery:**
```
Full-day premium clubs + Delivery/Pick-up = ฿1,700
(฿1,200 + ฿500 delivery)
```

##### 3. Second Men's Set (Conditional)
**Priority:** Medium | **Investment:** ฿20,000 | **Impact:** +฿5K/month

**Add if:** Current men's set utilization exceeds 50% of operating hours

**Rationale:** Men's demand typically 2-3x women's. Ensures availability during peak times and prevents lost sales.

#### ❌ NOT RECOMMENDED (Currently)

##### Left-Handed Premium Set
**Investment:** ฿20,000+ | **Expected demand:** 2-3 rentals/month

**Why not:**
- Left-handed golfers: ~10% of market
- At current volume, only 2-3 rentals/month expected
- Payback period: 6-12 months
- Capital better deployed elsewhere

**Alternative Strategy:**
- Continue charging premium for left-handed on-demand (e.g., ฿900)
- Partner with golf shop for left-handed referrals
- Revisit decision when seeing 5+ left-handed requests/month

## Part 6: Competitive Context

### Bangkok Indoor Golf Market

Based on market research, Bangkok's indoor golf market is growing rapidly:

- **Global golf simulator market:** 10.1% CAGR (USD 1.92B → USD 4.7B by 2034)
- **Thailand drivers:** Limited outdoor space, unpredictable weather, growing expat population
- **Key competitors:** PLP City Range (RBSC), Bravo Golf (Marriott), X-Golf, Golfzon venues

### Your Differentiation

LenGolf's "Social-Golfing" positioning attracts:
- Beginners who need equipment
- Casual players who don't own clubs
- Groups/events where convenience matters
- Tourists without their own gear

**This customer base is ideal for equipment rentals and accessories.**

### Competitor Strategies

| PLP City Range | Premium positioning | Limited retail |
