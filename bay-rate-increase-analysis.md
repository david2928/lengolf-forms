# Bay Rate Increase Analysis
## Revenue Impact of 50 THB/Hour Increase on Normal Bay Rates

**Analysis Period:** September - November 2025
**Date:** November 25, 2025

---

## Monthly Breakdown

| Month | Bookings | Hours | Additional Revenue (THB) | Status |
|-------|----------|-------|-------------------------|---------|
| **September** | 276 | 413 | 20,650 | Complete |
| **October** | 309 | 474 | 23,700 | Complete |
| **November** | 263 | 386 | 19,313 | Projected* |
| **Total** | **848** | **1,273** | **63,663** | |

*November projection based on 24 completed days (Nov 1-24), run rate applied to 30-day month.

---

## Key Findings

- **Q4 Total Impact:** 63,663 THB (~$1,768 USD) additional revenue
- **Monthly Average:** 21,221 THB per month
- **Strongest Month:** October (309 bookings, 23,700 THB)
- **November Trend:** 15% lower than October, tracking closer to September baseline

## Monthly Comparison
- October vs September: +12% bookings, +15% revenue
- November vs October: -15% bookings (projected)
- November vs September: -5% bookings (projected)

---

## Notes
- Analysis includes only "Normal Bay Rate" bookings
- Excludes package bookings, coaching sessions, ClassPass, and other booking types
- All cancelled bookings excluded from analysis
- 50 THB increase applied uniformly to all normal bay hours
