# Market Research Skill

Conducts competitive analysis and market research for Lengolf's golf simulator business in Bangkok.

## Business Context

**Lengolf Overview:**
- Golf simulator bay rental business in Bangkok, Thailand
- Competes with other golf simulators, driving ranges, and golf entertainment venues
- Target customers: Golf enthusiasts, corporate events, casual players

**Pricing Model:**
- Bay bookings: Hourly rates (standard and VIP bays)
- Coaching: Per-session rates with professional instructors
- Packages: Pre-paid bundles at discounted rates
- Club rentals: FREE with bay bookings (competitive advantage)

## Research Workflow

### Step 1: Define Research Scope
Ask about:
- Research focus (pricing, services, customer reviews, market trends)
- Specific competitors to analyze (or find top competitors)
- Geographic scope (Bangkok, Thailand, Southeast Asia)
- Time frame for trend analysis

### Step 2: Gather Data (Parallel Tracks)

**Track A: Competitor Pricing**
Research top golf simulator venues in Bangkok:
- Hourly bay rates
- Membership options
- Package pricing
- Additional services (coaching, F&B, equipment)

**Track B: Customer Sentiment**
Analyze reviews from:
- Google Reviews
- Facebook pages
- TripAdvisor
- Local Thai review sites

**Track C: Market Trends**
Research:
- Golf participation trends in Thailand
- Indoor golf/simulator market growth
- Technology trends (simulator brands, features)
- Seasonal demand patterns

### Step 3: Synthesize Findings

Present findings with:
- Confidence levels for each data point
- Clear source attribution
- Comparison to Lengolf's current positioning
- Actionable recommendations

## Key Competitors to Research

**Bangkok Golf Simulators:**
1. Search for "golf simulator bangkok"
2. Look for venues with similar offerings
3. Note pricing, services, and customer ratings

**Factors to Compare:**
- Bay rental rates (per hour)
- Simulator technology (TrackMan, GC, etc.)
- Number of bays
- Coaching availability
- F&B offerings
- Location/accessibility
- Operating hours
- Club rental policy (Lengolf advantage: FREE)

## Report Template

### Executive Summary
- Key findings (3-5 bullet points)
- Recommended actions

### Competitive Landscape
- Market position map
- Pricing comparison table
- Service comparison

### Market Opportunities
- Underserved segments

### Risks and Threats
